I am a Master's student in [Data Science](https://cds.nyu.edu/) at the [Center for Data Science](https://cds.nyu.edu/), [New York University](https://www.nyu.edu/) (expected May 2027). I also hold an M.S. in Social Policy and Applied Data Ethics from the [University of Pennsylvania](https://www.upenn.edu/) School of Social Policy & Practice, and a B.Econ. from [Xiamen University](https://www.xmu.edu.cn/) School of Economics.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **data science**, **urban policy**, and **social science**. I am particularly interested in using quantitative and computational methods to study transportation, urban governance, and economic development.

Currently, I work as a **Data Analyst** at the [NYU Financial Access Initiative (FAI)](https://www.financialaccess.org/) at the Wagner Graduate School of Public Service, and as a **Graduate Research Assistant** with [Prof. Zhan Guo](https://wagner.nyu.edu/community/faculty/zhan-guo) at NYU Wagner, where my research focuses on transportation policy and urban mobility.
