**Jan. 2023 – May 2024** | Xiamen, China

**Topic:** Rural Human Settlement Governance under the Rural Revitalization Initiative

- Secured the highest-level grant (~1,400 USD) through an independently authored project proposal on rural governance and regional planning under China's Rural Revitalization Initiative.
- Led a 6-member student research team and coordinated fieldwork across rural communities in Fujian Province, engaging with local residents, village committees, and township-level agencies.
- Designed and implemented a mixed-methods study combining fiscal analysis, resident interviews, and focus groups, to examine divergent priorities and perceptions between government agencies and local residents in regional planning processes.
- Analyzed governance challenges arising from mismatches between top-down policy objectives and bottom-up resident needs, including infrastructure investment, land use, and public service provision.
- Delivered a comprehensive research report providing policy recommendations on participatory planning and conflict mitigation for sustainable rural human settlement governance.
