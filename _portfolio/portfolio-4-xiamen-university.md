**Jan. 2022 – Jun. 2023** | Xiamen University, School of Economics, Xiamen, China

- Collaborated with faculty advisor across multiple research projects involving both qualitative and quantitative methods, focusing on regional development, migration, and labor outcomes.
- Conducted qualitative research by interviewing 30+ residents, facilitating focus groups, and conceptual framing, extending prior fieldwork on rural governance and regional planning into peer-reviewed academic manuscripts.
- Performed quantitative analysis using China Labor-force Dynamics Survey (CLDS) data in Stata, applying logistic regression to examine permanent urban settlement decisions among floating populations, with a focus on dialect barriers, social integration, and opportunity structures.
- Co-authored two peer-reviewed journal articles, both published in CSSCI-indexed Chinese journals, one in rural-urban studies and one in labor economics.
