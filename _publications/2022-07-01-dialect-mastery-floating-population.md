This paper examines the relationship between dialect mastery and permanent urban settlement decisions among China's floating population, using data from the China Labor-force Dynamics Survey (CLDS). Applying logistic regression methods, the study focuses on the roles of dialect barriers, social integration, and opportunity structures in shaping migration permanence.
